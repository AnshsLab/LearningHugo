I’m someone who learns best by building, exploring, and sometimes throwing myself headfirst into things just to see how they work. I’m naturally curious, a little restless, and always looking for something new to understand, improve, or create. Whether it’s code, design, Linux, open source, or just an idea that won’t leave me alone, I like the process of turning curiosity into something real.

Most of my growth has come from doing things directly. Trying, failing, fixing, and doing it again until it finally clicks. Over time, that has led me deeper into web development, open source, Linux, and the kind of work that lets both logic and creativity exist in the same space.

I work with tools and technologies like **C++**, **Python**, **HTML**, **CSS**, **JavaScript**, **Hugo**, **Git**, **Linux**, **Express**, **React**, and **MongoDB**. I enjoy building on the web, understanding systems properly, and learning in a way that feels hands-on instead of purely theoretical.

Outside of development, I’m also drawn to ideas, stories, and the quieter things that leave a mark over time. This space exists somewhere between all of that. A little personal, a little technical, and always evolving.
